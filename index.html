About

Hi! Thanks for visiting my page. Last updated Sept 15, 2023.
I am a first-year PhD Student in Computer Science at Columbia University. I focus on NLP and am advised by Prof. Smaranda Muresan. Boradly, my research focuses on creating language models that are helpful to humans:

  • How can we utilize human-AI collaboration to build high-quality, challenging datasets and utilize expert feedback to improve AI models? [1,2,5,6]
  • How can we teach the models not only to predict the labels, but also to explain their answer? [2,5,6]
  • How can we ensure language models correctly interpret nuances of langauge, such as metaphors and idioms? [2,3]
  • How can we use language models to assist humans in large-scale tasks requiring sophisticated reasoning, like fact-checking? [4]
